The Curator

Sandile is an emerging Nairobi-based concert curator focused on Afrohouse, Afrotech, and electronic African music culture.

Our events bring together the city’s most exciting DJs and audiences within immersive nightlife environments that celebrate the energy of contemporary African electronic music.

Our Story

The Sandile Journey

Event 01

Sandile Sundowner

The Sandile journey began with an intimate sunset gathering titled Sandile Sundowner hosted at Park Inn by Radisson.

The event blended daytime atmosphere with evening Afrohouse dancefloor energy, bringing together a niche but passionate community of music lovers discovering the sound of Afrohouse in Nairobi.

Event 02

B2B in the Jungle

Sandile later partnered with 720 Music Lab to host B2B in the Jungle at KODA Nairobi.

The event focused on collaborative DJ culture, featuring back-to-back performances where artists shared the stage simultaneously.

Headliners: BigNyagz Tina Ador
Sandile's in-house DJ: GorillaFace
Event 03

Sandile One Year Anniversary

Sandile celebrated its one-year anniversary with its most ambitious event yet.

South African artist Sykes headlined the night at KODA Nairobi, drawing a sold-out crowd of approximately 800 attendees.

The event marked a major milestone, demonstrating the growth of Sandile’s Afrohouse community within Nairobi.

Through these events, Sandile has organically built a growing community of Afrohouse and Afrotech music lovers within Nairobi’s nightlife and cultural landscape.

The platform continues to evolve as a curator of music experiences rather than a single event.
