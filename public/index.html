Introduction

Sandile is an emerging Nairobi-based concert curator focused on Afrohouse, Afrotech, and electronic African music culture.

Our events bring together the city's most exciting DJs and audiences within immersive nightlife environments that celebrate the energy of contemporary African electronic music.

Our next production, Avengers Assemble, represents our most ambitious concept yet.

We invite Smirnoff to become the official spirit partner powering this experience.

The Event – Avengers Assemble

A 12-hour Afrohouse DJ marathon where 20–25 DJs perform back-to-back in pairs, creating collaborative sonic exchanges throughout the night.

Each DJ pair forms an Avenger team, contributing to a musical narrative that builds from sunset to sunrise.
